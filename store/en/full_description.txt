Exacta is a tamper-proof photo recording tool built for field engineers, construction inspectors, real-estate agents, insurance adjusters, and logistics workers.

Time, GPS, and address are burned directly into the photo pixels at capture time. Every photo is mathematically linked to the next via a SHA-256 hash chain. Tampering with even a single byte is detected instantly.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
■ Key Features
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

▸ Pixel-Burned Stamps
Time, date, address, GPS coordinates, altitude, compass heading, and speed are composited directly into the image pixels. EXIF metadata removal cannot erase them.

▸ NTP Time Sync
Capture time is synchronized with internet standard time servers. Manipulating the device clock has no effect on the recorded time.

▸ SHA-256 Hash Chain (Tamper Detection)
Each photo is mathematically linked to the previous one. Tampering with a single photo breaks the chain for all subsequent photos and is detected immediately.

▸ Secure Capture Mode
Completely blocks GPS and address recording, and strips EXIF location data. Safe for sensitive sites (military, medical, legal, etc.).

▸ Project-Based Organization
Create projects and auto-classify photos. Bulk share by project, color labels, active/done state management.

▸ Legal Evidence Pack PDF Export
Enter case name and author → automatically generates a PDF with cover page, all photos, hashes, and external verification instructions. Ready for court, insurance, or dispute submission.

▸ Field-Friendly UI
Bold text, large shutter button (66dp), 56dp touch targets for gloved hands. Designed for outdoor direct sunlight and one-handed operation.

▸ Fully Offline
No server communication. No ads. No accounts. Photos are stored only on your device.

▸ Multilingual
한국어 / English / 日本語

■ Data Protection
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Exacta does not transmit a single byte of user data externally.
All photos, metadata, and hash chains are stored only on your device. Data leaves the device only when you explicitly tap the share button.
