**Professional Stamp Camera for Field Workers**

Exacta is a time/location stamp camera designed for field engineers, construction technicians, inspection officers, and security personnel. Every photo is pixel-burned with time, date, GPS coordinates, and address — creating reliable photo records that hold up as legal evidence.
